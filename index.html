What We Do

One call. A fully monitored jobsite.

AI Camera Trailers

Solar & battery-powered mobile trailers with AI-powered PTZ cameras that detect and auto-track people or vehicles in real time. Deployed to your location within 24 hours.

  • AI smart detection — filters out animals, weather & false alarms
  • PTZ camera auto-tracks movement, zooms in automatically
  • Fully solar — no generator, no fuel runs

After-Hours Monitoring

Our live monitoring team covers your site from end of workday until your crew is back on-site — backed by AI detection and push alerts that go straight to us, not your phone. No app, no live feed for you to manage. You only hear from us if there's a real incident.

  • After-hours live-agent monitoring, fully handled by our team
  • AI auto-tracking plus manual PTZ control during active incidents
  • Incident report & police coordination if something real happens
