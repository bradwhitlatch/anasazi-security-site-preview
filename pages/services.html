Core Service

AI-Powered PTZ Camera Trailers

A rugged, self-contained watchtower for your site. Every trailer runs an AI-powered PTZ (pan-tilt-zoom) camera that automatically detects and tracks people or vehicles, then zooms in for a clear look — not a fixed lens hoping something walks into frame. Solar and battery powered, deployed and pointed within 30 minutes of arrival.

  • AI smart detection — filters out animals, weather & false alarms
  • PTZ camera auto-tracks motion and zooms in automatically
  • Telescoping mast up to 20 ft for full-site line of sight
  • Motion-activated floodlights and audible siren deterrent
  • 100% solar-powered — works off-grid, no fuel runs
  • Weatherproof for desert heat, wind, and winter cold
Core Service

24/7 Remote Monitoring

A camera is only as good as the eyes watching it. Our monitoring team watches your feed live, around the clock — push alerts and live tracking go straight to us, not to your phone. There's no app for you to install and no feed for you to watch. You only hear from us if something real happens.

  • Live-agent monitoring, 24/7 — fully handled by our team, internally
  • Confirmed incidents are reported to you directly, with details
  • Direct police coordination on confirmed incidents
  • Recorded footage retained and searchable if an incident occurs
