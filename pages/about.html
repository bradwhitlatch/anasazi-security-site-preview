About Us

Built in Southern Utah. Watching Southern Utah.

We started Anasazi Security because too many local builders were losing tools, materials, and equipment to theft — and the surveillance options available either had no local presence or left them to babysit an app all night.

Our Story

Named for what came before us.

The Anasazi were watchers of the land long before any of us — builders who understood this terrain and respected it. We named our company after that legacy because it's exactly what we do: keep watch over the ground people are working hard to build on, day and night, through desert heat and winter cold.

Anasazi Security is based right here in St. George, not routed through a call center somewhere else. When you call, you're talking to someone who knows this region's jobsites, terrain, and the kind of theft and vandalism that actually happens here.
